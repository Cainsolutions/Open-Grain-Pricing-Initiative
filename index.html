Open Grain Pricing Initiative

A free Excel tool helping grain growers understand their cost per tonne, break-even price, and harvest margin — built for small to medium agribusiness.

Free & Non-Commercial

About This Tool

The Open Grain Pricing Initiative is a non-profit project created to give grain growers access to the same margin clarity that large agribusinesses take for granted. The model is built in Excel and designed to be straightforward — enter your costs, yield, and current price, and the tool does the rest.


Whether you are managing a single crop paddock or planning across a season, this tool helps you make calm, numbers-based decisions at the bin rather than relying on gut feel under harvest pressure.

What the Tool Does

  • Calculates cost per tonne from your input costs and yield
  • Shows your margin per tonne at current market prices
  • Models warehousing and storage costs over time
  • Provides a quick calculator for any tonnage and sale price
  • Breaks down costs by input line across your crop acreage
  • Locked formulas to prevent accidental edits

How to Use

  • Step 1 — Download the Excel file using the button below
  • Step 2 — Open the file and enable editing if prompted
  • Step 3 — Enter your total acres and crop acreage in the yellow input cells
  • Step 4 — Enter your input costs — seed, fertiliser, chemical, and urea
  • Step 5 — Enter your tonnes harvested and current market price
  • Step 6 — Review your cost per tonne, margin per tonne, and profit figures
  • Step 7 — Use the warehousing section to model holding costs over time

All yellow-highlighted cells are inputs. All other cells are calculated automatically — do not edit them.
